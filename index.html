Got questions?

Everything you need to know

Is UNIFY free?

Yes — completely free for all students. No subscriptions, no premium tiers. UNIFY is funded through student organisation partnerships, not student wallets.

How do you verify students?

Sign up with your school email or upload your student ID. We verify before your account goes live — keeping the community genuine and scam-free.

Is this only for universities?

No. UNIFY covers SHS, technical institutes, teacher training colleges, and JHS students navigating BECE placement. If you're a student in Ghana, there's a hub for you.

Will it work on slow internet?

Yes — that's a core design goal. The app is under 5MB and tested on 2G in Navrongo and Wa. We compress images, cache aggressively, and never autoplay video.

Is my data safe?

Your phone number is never public. Chats are encrypted. We never sell your data. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time.

When will my school be available?

We're rolling out school-by-school starting with KNUST, UG, UCC, and PRESEC. Join the waitlist and you'll be notified within 2–4 weeks when your school goes live.
