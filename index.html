Features

Built different.
Built for Ghana.

Not another copy of a Western app. Every feature on UNIFY was designed around the real Ghanaian student experience.

🏫

School Hubs — your campus, online

Every school in Ghana gets its own dedicated hub. A living feed for your school's announcements, events, resources, and conversations — filtered by your cohort and interests.

School feeds Year-group groups Pinned posts Interest tags
🏠

Roommate Matching

Answer a few questions about your lifestyle and we'll connect you with compatible roommates before you arrive on campus.

Compatibility quiz Hostel-specific
💬

Direct Messaging

Secure DMs and group chats. Connect one-on-one or create study rooms with your coursemates.

DMs Group chats Study rooms
📚

Academic Resources

Share and discover past questions, notes, and study guides. Ask seniors who've aced the same exams.

Past questions Peer tutoring

Ghana-First Features

Post-BECE placement guides, WASSCE prep communities, and hostel reviews — built for the uniquely Ghanaian student journey.

BECE guide WASSCE prep Placement help

Works on Any Data

Optimized for Ghana's networks. Runs on 2G, 3G, or slow WiFi — because great tools shouldn't need great internet.

Low bandwidth Offline support
How it works

From signup to connected
in under 3 minutes

No lengthy setup. Join, find your school, and start connecting with your people right away.

1

Create your profile

Sign up with your phone number or email. Verify your student status and add your school, year group, and interests.

2

Join your school hub

Pick your school from 180+ listings across Ghana — SHS, technical institutes, and universities all included.

3

Connect and thrive

Meet students, join communities, find a roommate, swap notes, and make the most of your school years.
