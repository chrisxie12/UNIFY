Features

Everything students need

A complete platform designed around the Ghanaian student experience — not a copy of something built elsewhere.

🔐

Identity & Onboarding

Get verified as a Ghanaian student in seconds. No bureaucracy — just your phone, email, or Google.

  • Phone, Email, or Google sign-up
  • School placement verification
  • Customizable profile with interests
  • Cohort & year-group tagging
🏫

School Hubs (Unit Pages)

Every school has its own hub — a living feed for announcements, questions, events, and more.

  • Dedicated page per school
  • Filtered tags and pinned posts
  • Interest-based sub-communities
  • SHS & university hubs
💬

Messaging & Connection

Find students who share your interests and connect — whether they're in your school or across the country.

  • Secure direct messaging (DM)
  • Group chats & study rooms
  • Interest-based matching
  • Plan meetups & events
📚

Academic Support

Share notes, ask questions, and get help from seniors who've been through the same exams.

  • BECE & WASSCE resource library
  • Course-specific Q&A boards
  • Peer tutoring connections
  • Past questions & study guides
🏠

Ghana-Specific Tools

Features you won't find anywhere else — built specifically for the Ghanaian student journey.

  • Post-BECE school placement guide
  • Hostel roommate matching
  • WASSCE & university admission info
  • Campus accommodation reviews
📶

Low-Bandwidth First

Designed to work on 2G and 3G networks — because great connectivity shouldn't be a prerequisite.

  • Lightweight mobile-first design
  • Offline reading support
  • Data-efficient media handling
  • Works on any Android device
How it works

Up and running in 3 steps

No complicated setup — join, find your school, and start connecting with your people.

1

Create your profile

Sign up with your phone or email, verify your student status, and set up your profile in under 2 minutes.

2

Join your school hub

Find your school from over 180 listings across Ghana — SHS, technical schools, and universities.

3

Connect & grow

Explore your school feed, join interest groups, message peers, and make the most of your student years.
