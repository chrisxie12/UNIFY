Core MVP Features

Identity & Onboarding

Simple sign-up with OTP, email, or Google, plus customizable student profiles and school placement selection.

Unit Pages

School hubs with central feeds and sub-communities for programs, year groups, and hostel roommate search.

Connection & Messaging

Student discovery and secure one-on-one messaging to plan meetups, share insights, and support each other.

Our Vision

UNIFY aims to be the central hub for the Ghanaian student experience—from post-BECE placements to navigating university life. We are building a low-bandwidth, accessible, mobile-first platform where every student can find their tribe.

Roadmap & Call to Action

We are actively building UNIFY in phases:

  1. Foundation: schemas, authentication, and core platform setup
  2. Profiles & Units: school communities and personalization
  3. Social Mechanics: posting, comments, and direct messaging
  4. Ghana-Specific Features: cohort support and housing connections
